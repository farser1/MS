Questions and Answers

I’ve left my name... What happens next?
We email out free trial invitations every Saturday so that you can get ready to start the programme on a Monday. Be sure to add “hello@joinmyteam.co” to your contacts to make sure it doesn't go to your spam folder.

What happens after my trial?
At the end of your trial, you can upgrade to a subscription to continue using your MyTeam. If you don't want to subscribe, then your account will be put on hold.

Do I need to hand over my credit card details to start my free trial?
We won’t ask you for any payment information until you have completed your free trial and want to sign up.

Am I tied to a contract?
There are no contracts at MyTeam you can cancel or pause your account at any time. 

When subscribing, what payment methods do you accept?
Payment can be made by card or direct debit. Payments are taken weekly and can be cancelled any time.

Who do I contact for help?
Throughout your program you will be able to ask your coach any questions through the community. You can also email John and Chris (the co-founders) at any time before or after joining at hello@joinmyteam.co. We’d love to hear from you.
