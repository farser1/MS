Meet MyTeam

Making fitness simple, affordable and fun!

Working out at home used to mean watching the same boring DVD again and again. MyTeam channels connect people directly with the best trainers around the world. Now you can experience a new workout every single week from your living room, the gym, a park, a hotel room — wherever you are! It’s like being able to take your favourite gym class and instructor with you wherever you go.
