Meet MyTeam

Our Mission

We make getting into shape simple and affordable. By applying behavioural change principles we drive sustainable healthy living rather than short term results. Instead of following a static plan downloaded from the internet our members are led through a progressive programme which adapts to their lifestyle and receive unlimited support.

Our story started in 2013 when co-founders John & Chris joined forces with weight loss specialist Paul. Combining offline coaching skills and fitness expertise with a totally online platform, we created a powerful weight loss system which doesn't require dieting, calorie counting, or expensive equipment.

Each week members get up to six actions to complete. Simple things, like completing workouts, learning about health topics or implementing small lifestyle changes. Breaking 12 weeks down into manageable steps allows members to focus on each day - because all the planning and thinking has been done for them.

We’ve just launched our weight loss programme and we’d love you to take part. To learn more about us, you can read about the differences between online and offline training, why we think personal training is effective but too expensive and our thoughts on keeping fitness simple on our blog.
