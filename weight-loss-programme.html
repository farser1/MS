Meet the coach

Hey, I’m Paul, the weight loss coach for MyTeam, and it’s my mission to help you reach your goal weight! I’m always available if you have a question or want some progress feedback during the programme. I also manage the online community where everyone can share how they’re going and support each other as a team.

After completing my degree in Sport and Exercise Science I started working for a reputable gym, only to discover just how few people ever achieved their fitness goals in this environment. I believe it’s due to the lack of information provided, particularly on nutrition, and wasted time on unnecessary equipment and exercises. This was the motivating factor for me to move into fitness coaching, and ultimately, helping to create MyTeam.

When I’m not in the MyTeam community, you’ll probably find me rock climbing or working as a Research Exercise Scientist at Great Ormond Street Hospital, London, helping kids with Cystic Fibrosis get active!

Helping people achieve life changing results is what I live for, and I’d love to help you too! 

Questions and Answers

How much weight can I expect to lose?
Members lose up to 4.5kg in the first four weeks. A safe weight loss target is around 1kg per week, so you might expect to lose up to 12kg over twelve weeks. Results will vary based on your starting weight, how closely you follow the programme and your body.

What will I be eating?
You get to choose what you want to eat. Our healthy eating habits help you focus on eating the right foods. Our growing recipe library offers you a large variety of meal & snack suggestions. Recipes are available for vegetarians, vegans, and gluten intolerance.

How much exercise will I be expected to do?
Workouts are designed to be short and efficient, taking between 10 and 25 minutes each and catering for all fitness levels without fancy equipment. You can do them at home, outdoors or at the gym. We recommend three sessions a week. As you progress new workouts are unlocked and you choose when to increase the intensity.

What if I can’t exercise or have injuries?
You can just follow the nutrition part of the programme. Nutrition is a huge part of the weight loss puzzle on it's own, so you can still get great results. 

What happens if I miss or don't complete a week?
The programme is self paced, if you miss a week you can pick up where you left off when you are ready to continue. If you don't complete a week to your satisfaction you can always repeat it.

What happens after 12 weeks?
You can continue your membership after you have completed the programme. You'll get complete access to the video workouts and recipes, and you’ll be able to access the community and coaching team.
