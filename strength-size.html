Josh O'Leary

My passion for strength and conditioning started when playing competitive rugby at school and university. Getting stronger and faster was a necessity for me in order to play at my best. At university I studied Biology and learnt about Exercise Physiology, giving me the theoretical knowledge to match my passion. 

As a scientist I strive to apply a scientific approach to training. I seek to understand why and how different exercises and nutritional changes effect our bodies. I'm always happy to go into this detail with clients who want a more in depth understanding of fitness. 

Immediately following university I began working for one of the large banks. The best part of my day was when I would train some of my colleagues during lunch. After a year of this I decided to follow my real passion and retrained as a Personal Trainer. Since then I've been lucky enough to train clients internationally both in London and now in Brisbane, Australia where I work for a private fitness studio. 

I believe that with a solid evidence based program, support and the right motivation anyone can reach their goals and I am here to help you reach yours.
