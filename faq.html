Questions and Answers

What is a MyTeam workout?
A MyTeam workout it just like going to a weekly fitness class except it’s pre-recorded. You can do it in the privacy of your own home, at your convenience, saving you time and money. If you ever have any questions about the workout, just leave a comment below the video and your trainer will get back to you.

What is a MyTeam channel?
A channel is run by a trainer who releases one workout every week of a similar difficulty and duration tailored to help you reach a specific goal.

What technology do I need?
All you need is a working internet connection and a computer, tablet or smartphone to get going!

How much does MyTeam cost?
Subscribing to a MyTeam channel costs £5 a month, and you can cancel anytime.

How long is a MyTeam workout?
Workout length varies from channel to channel and from week to week. We are focused on efficiency and depending on the channel workouts are normally between 10-30 minutes including a warm up.

How hard are MyTeam workouts?
Each channel provides workouts tailored a specific fitness level as listed. Within specific workouts trainers will also offer easier and harder variations of exercises.

How many times will I workout each week?
Each channel has a recommended workout frequency set by the trainer (normally 2-4 workouts a week). If you’re already doing some exercise each week, you can always substitute one of your sessions for a video and vice versa.

What fitness equipment do I need?
Our focus is on equipment free home workouts. You just press play and follow the instructions from your trainer. If a channel does require any equipment it will specify this.

What will I be eating?
Some channels come with additional resources and eating guides. Your trainer will also be happy to respond to questions and point you in the direction of good articles and resources.

What happens if I miss or don't complete a week?
Every workout gets added to an archive of workouts which you can always access. This means if you miss a week you can always go back and do the workout when you can. It also means you’ll always have access to all the previous workouts so you can repeat your favourites.

Who do I contact for help?
You will be able to ask your trainer any questions. You can also email John and Chris (the co-founders) at any time before or after joining at hello@joinmyteam.co. We’d love to hear from you.
